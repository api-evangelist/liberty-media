# Liberty Media (liberty-media)

Liberty Media Corporation is a media and entertainment holding company headquartered in Englewood, Colorado, owning interests in a portfolio of assets across media, entertainment, and sports. Its primary holding is the Formula One Group, which encompasses Formula 1 racing operations and MotoGP motorcycle racing, alongside investments in companies such as Liberty Broadband, QVC Group, Atlanta Braves Holdings, and Liberty Live Holdings.
